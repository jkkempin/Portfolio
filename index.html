Projects

Fossil Quiz App

Test your knowledge of dinosaur fossils. This app was inspired by my 4 year old son who is on a big dinosaur kick and is a great app for anyone looking to learn something new about dinosaur fossils. This 5 question quiz does not allow the user to move on until they chose an answer for each question, gives them feedback when they chose the wrong answer, and resets the quiz if they would like to try again. The app tracks and displays the user's score on each question.

Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery
